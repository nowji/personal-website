MIAMI TRIP SUMMER 2026

Day 1 Monday 6/8

  • Brightline from Orlando to Miami (9:20 AM- 12:54 PM) (~$100 round trip)
  • Go to hotel to drop off bags
  • Miami Beach (Route 100 Metrobus)
    • Have lunch
    • Chill on beach
    • Go to South Pointe Park
    • Walk around Lincoln Road Mall and Art Deco District
  • Go back to hotel to check in and shower
  • Spend the night at Brickell (dinner, drinks, etc.)

Day 2 Tuesday 6/9

  • Bill Baggs State Park (Route 26 Metrobus) (leave around 9:30 AM)
    • Lots of hiking trails
    • Great beach
    • Old lighthouse
  • Lunch at Key Biscane
  • Go back to hotel to shower and change
  • Explore Little Havana (Route 7 Metrobus)
  • Marlins Game (6:40 PM) (Easy to get tickets <$50)
    •

Day 3 Wednesday 6/10

  • Breakfast and walk around at Wynmood (free trolley) (lots of cool buildings and murals)
  • Walk around Miami Design District (upscale neighborhood with expensive retail)
  • Night to go clubbing (TBD where)

Day 4 Thursday 6/11

  • Check out of hotel and have them hold our bags
  • Go to Museum Park Station
    • Frost Science Museum (Aquarium, planetarium, science exhibits) ($30 ticket)
    • Pérez Art Museum Miami ($14 student ticket)
  • Have an early dinner / late lunch by Museum Park or Downtown
  • Grab bags from hotel
  • Go to Brightline station (depart 6:45 PM)
